# Task Buddy Privacy Policy

*Last updated: December 12, 2025*

## Overview

Task Buddy is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

## Data Storage

All your tasks, tags, and settings are stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if enabled). Your data is synced directly between your devices using Apple's iCloud service. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No usage data
- No personal information

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud container. This data is:
- Encrypted by Apple
- Accessible only to you
- Not visible to us or any third party

## Third-Party Services

Task Buddy does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Task Buddy does not knowingly collect any information from children.
